A well developed and functioning civil registration system ensures the registration of all vital events including births, and deaths and issues relevant certificates as proof of such registration. Civil registration promotes efficient government planning, effective use of resources and aid. And more accurate monitoring of progress towards achieving the sustainable development plan.
Child born registration, an effective and efficient birth registration system is critical both as a fundamental right and a strong device to wars promoting the wellbeing of children and their protection against all forms of violence and deprivation. International law, regional and the national legal frameworks as well as scholarly articles acknowledge that the child’s registration immediately after birth guarantees the rights from birth to a name, nationality, family relations and all the related privileges

About us

The procedure of recording a child's birth is known as birth registration.
It establishes a legal record of where the child was born and who his or her parents are, at the very least. A child's birth certificate - his or her first legal proof of identity – requires birth registration.
